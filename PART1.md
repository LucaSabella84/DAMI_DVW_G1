**The Great War[https://www.poppyfield.org/] 
*Cemetary

Summary 
This particular visualization provides information about the wars that have occured since the begining of the 20st century, describes countries that have been involved in a particular conflict, the duration of conflicts and the number of casualties. The data is visualized in a graph way, describing the duration on the Y-axis, which is represented logarithmicly in order to demonstrate the high difference in duration (approx. 6 months - 65 years), while on the X-axis the timeline from 1899 untill 2014 is mentioned.  The root of the poppy is at the beginning of the war, while the head is at the end of the war. Additionaly, the size of the head of poppies illustrate the amount of war casualties: the higher the number the bigger the head. By clicking on a certain poppy, it is possible to study a certain conflict in details. The conflicts can be filtered by a number of casualties and time period.
